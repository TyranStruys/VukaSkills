Welcome to VukaSkills

With a 43% youth unemployment rate in South Africa, VukaSkills steps in with gamified training and job matching to empower 18-30-year-olds. Join us in building a brighter future!

About Us

We’re a startup applying to the Oppenheimer Startup Club SA, driven by a mission to reduce unemployment through innovative technology. VukaSkills combines skills development with opportunity, targeting South Africa’s youth.

Our Features

RIASEC Profiling

Take our Holland RIASEC quiz to find your fit—Realistic, Investigative, Artistic, Social, Enterprising, or Conventional—and unlock personalized career paths.

Fishbone Fixer

Master the Fishbone Diagram in our gamified module to solve workplace challenges, earning a Problem-Solver Badge that boosts your credentials.

Job Matching

Get connected to jobs tailored to your RIASEC profile, with virtual mentorship to guide you every step of the way.
